Being a Web Dev

This journey could be backdated to 2005 when Tim created his first html website. Built using Adobe Dreamweaver, Tim learned the ins and outs of creating a basic website and had it published for his first business SerVAll; a grocery shopping and delivery company in Lafayette, LA.

Tim then built several other websites for various projects and businesses since 2005. All these websites allowed clients to see products, services and submit inquiries for appointments and send emails to Tim.

Starting October 11, 2021, Tim has begun his official studies with a reputable coding bootcamp and is learning to build upon his current html and css knowledge with the add elements of javascript functionality.
